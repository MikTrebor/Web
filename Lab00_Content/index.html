Julius Caesar

Timeline

  • July 13, 100 BCE: Caesar is born
  • 84 BCE: Marries Cornelia
  • 72 BCE: Serves as tribune
  • 69 BCE: Serves as quaestor
  • 67 BCE: Marries Pompeia
  • 65 BCE: Serves as aedile
  • 63 BCE: Serves as Pontifex Maximus
  • 62 BCE: Serves as Praetor
  • 61 BCE: Serves as proconsul of Hispania
  • 60 BCE: First Triumvirate
  • 59 BCE: Serves as consul
  • 59 BCE: Pompey marries Julia
  • 59 BCE: Marries Calpurnia
  • 58-52 BCE: Serves as proconsul of Gaul
  • 52 BCE: Defeats Gauls at Battle of Alesia
  • 49 BCE: Crosses Rubicon with his army- begins civil war
  • 48 BCE: Serves as consul
  • 48 BCE: Defeats Pompey at Battle of Pharsalus
  • 46 BCE: Appointed Dictator of Rome
  • March 15, 44 BCE: Caesar is assassinated

Quotes

I came, I saw, I conquered"

"Gallia est omnis divisa in partes tres"
"All Gaul is divided into three parts"

"Carthago delenda est"
"Carthage must be destroyed"
